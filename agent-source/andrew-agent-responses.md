## PUB OF THE FUTURE
> Triggers: pub of the future, pub, design week, old bar, third space

### General
In 2020 I co-authored 'The Pub of the Future' as part of Melbourne Design Week, with the Craig Walker team. We held a workshop at The Old Bar in Fitzroy — two days before COVID shut everything down, which made the whole thing feel pretty prescient.

We explored three themes: the pub as the heart of community and a vital 'third space', the tension between commerce, community and culture, and the pub's role as a cultural ecosystem.

The key insight was that pubs don't need to be designed to be perfect. Their opportunity is to embrace, update, rethink and redefine what they've always been: public houses where people congregate and connect.
